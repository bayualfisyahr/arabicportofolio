Background & Expertise

I am a graduate of Ma'had Al Imarat combined with a technical background from Vocational High School (SMK) majoring in Software Engineering.

Currently, I actively teach at SDIT Al Maahyrah & MDTA Daarut Tafaqquh (Elementary Level Islamic Schools). My primary focus is creating engaging Learning Experiences for children through digital gamification, while maintaining the essence of proper Arabic grammar.
